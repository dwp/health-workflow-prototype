Status alignment Confluence documentation

Version 1

This prototype looks at status alignment to match the tracker in the HTA, to reduce cognitive load on the users. It allows a user to do the following:

  • Update status, but with a status set that matches the tracker
  • filter by new selection of statuses
  • View new statuses on caseload without the coloured tag
  • Entry into the update status page is by find a claimant (no name or NINO required, just hit search. Or the top record in any caseload (status lists don't work)
